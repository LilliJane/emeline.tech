Who I am

I am a 26-year-old Software Engineer. I graduated from Epitech with a master's degree, and I studied sustainable and service design at The Hague University of Applied Sciences in the Netherlands, where I also co-redacted an essay about Natural Language Processing.
I have two main interests: distributed systems and machine learning (from a software engineer perspective). My experiences are all about back-end engineering using Kubernetes, public cloud (AWS, GCP), Go and Python.
I worked at Nerdalize as a Software Engineer, and I was the co-founder of a French neobank. I previously worked at Epitech as an IT program manager. I'm now looking for a new job to continue working on my technical skills.
